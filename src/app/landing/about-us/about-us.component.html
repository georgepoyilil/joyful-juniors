About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Joyful Juniors International Preschool and Daycare, where we believe that every child’s early years should be filled with joy, learning, and growth. Located in the heart of Thavakkara, Kannur, we provide a nurturing environment that encourages children to explore, discover, and develop a lifelong love for learning.

Our Approach

At Joyful Juniors, we understand that each child is unique, and our carefully designed curriculum reflects this belief. Our international approach combines the best of modern early childhood education methods with a focus on creativity, play-based learning, and holistic development. Whether it’s building social skills, fostering creativity, or developing cognitive abilities, we ensure that every child has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Facilities

We offer state-of-the-art facilities that prioritize safety, comfort, and development. Our classrooms are fully air-conditioned, well-ventilated, and equipped with age-appropriate learning materials to enhance your child's learning experience. With real-time CCTV access, parents can rest assured knowing their little ones are in safe hands.

Flexible Programs

We cater to the diverse needs of parents with flexible timing options, including our popular "Drop and Shop" service. Whether you need full-day care, part-time hours, or just a few hours while you run errands, we’ve got you covered.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a warm, engaging, and supportive atmosphere where children are inspired to learn, express themselves, and become confident individuals. We are committed to partnering with parents to ensure that their child’s early education journey is a joyful and rewarding experience.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced and caring staff
  • Safe and secure environment
  • Age-appropriate programs for each group
  • Focus on play-based learning
  • Bright and fun facilities
